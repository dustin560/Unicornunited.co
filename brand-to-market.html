The Framework

Three phases.
One clear path to market

Each phase builds on the last. You only move forward when the evidence says you should.

Phase 1
Brand Definition
Establish your positioning, messaging, and creative direction — then validate it with a real audience before spending on campaigns.
  • Start Your Story brand game
  • Positioning statement and messaging
  • Creative concept development
  • Panel survey with message testing
  • Product vs creative diagnostic
Gate → Client approves positioning + survey confirms direction
Phase 2
Campaign Validation
Build the channels, produce the creative, and test with real audiences. Paid media validates whether the positioning holds in the real world.
  • Landing page build
  • Social channel setup and management
  • Creative variant production
  • Paid campaign testing
  • Validation scorecard and recommendation
Gate → Validation score determines whether to launch or refine
Phase 2b
Launch Activation
Convert validation momentum directly into your first sale. Conditional on strong validation — the channels are already live, the audience is already warm.
  • E-commerce or booking setup
  • Launch campaign creative
  • Email nurture sequence
  • Paid promotion of launch
  • Post-launch analysis and final scorecard
Conditional → Only activates on strong validation score

Every phase is evidence-led. We score validation across seven behavioural signals — from landing page conversion and ad engagement to purchase intent and sell-through rate. You always know exactly where you stand and what to do next.
