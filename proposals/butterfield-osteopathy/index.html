The Meeting

The central organising idea of the brand. Where practitioner and patient meet — and what changes between them. Not a marketing concept. A clinical philosophy that happens to make exceptional marketing.

The Threshold Device

The brand’s signature creative framework: an above/below line device that captures the patient journey. ‘What you arrived carrying’ above the line. ‘What becomes possible’ below. Used across every touchpoint from social media to the treatment room door.

Visual Identity

Logo, colour palette (white, linen, warm grey, stone, oak, terracotta, sage, ink), and a two-typeface system — Jost for structural precision, Cormorant Garamond for literary warmth.

Tone of Voice

Precise, warm, never precious. Written like the best literary non-fiction: specific, empathetic, structurally clear. Never clinical. Never wellness. Every sentence should make someone feel seen.

Practitioner Engagement

A complete strategy for bringing the team on the journey — from WhatsApp messaging to brand induction to a monthly rhythm of prompts. Practitioners as co-authors, not recipients.

Marketing-Comms Plan
