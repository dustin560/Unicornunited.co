Brand Definition

Starts with Start Your Story — played individually as a brand definition game. The outputs feed into positioning, messaging, and creative concept development, including 3–4 creative variants for the panel survey.

Panel Survey with Message Testing

A 200+ response survey on Pollfish, Millennial-weighted (60%+), with four sections: blind product test, creative variant ranking, brand story reveal, and demographics. The survey separates product desirability from creative performance — so if something underperforms, we know what to fix.

First Drop Activation

The channels are already live. The audience is already warm. Phase 2b converts that momentum directly into the first 50-unit drop via Shopify. Includes launch campaign creative, email nurture sequence (teaser, launch day, last chance), and coordinated social coverage.

Why It's Conditional

If validation is weak (below 2.5), spending more on launch won't fix the underlying issue. The diagnostic tells you whether the problem is the product or the creative. Fix the right thing first. The first 50 units are themselves the final validation signal — sell-through rate is the highest-weighted metric in the scorecard.
