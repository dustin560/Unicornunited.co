Start Your Story Workshop

We kick off with a 1-hour group session where the founding team plays Start Your Story — Unicorn's brand positioning game. Not a slideshow. A fast-moving, structured exercise where you collectively define who you are, what you believe, who you're for, and what makes you different.

The session produces a raw positioning artefact. We compare and discuss the results to co-define the brand manifesto. Where you agree is the backbone. Where you disagree is what we test.

Desk Research + Validation

Rapid research covering the ND fashion landscape, competitive mapping, market sizing, and Gen Z values-driven consumption. Then we test through a CSM student focus group and a competition-led survey distributed through neurodivergent communities — both using a blind-then-reveal methodology.
