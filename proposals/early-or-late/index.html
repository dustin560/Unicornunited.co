Landing Page as Survey

We build a branded Early or Late landing page that doubles as the research instrument. Visitors experience the brand the way a real customer would — scrolling through the artwork, reading the story, seeing the price. Embedded interactions capture the data: artwork ranking (creative direction), purchase intent after price reveal (pricing validation), and email signup (warm audience for Phase 2). Behavioural signals — scroll depth, time on page, click patterns — add a layer of data you can't get from a traditional questionnaire.

Blended Traffic (Panel + Organic)

Drive ~100 visitors through a mix of a small paid Pollfish panel (50–70 demographically targeted, unbiased respondents) and organic distribution through your network (30–50 via Instagram, WhatsApp, contacts). The page is designed with forced-choice ranking and price anchoring to offset response bias from organic reach. At the end of Phase 1, you know which artwork to lead with, which messages land, and whether the £150 price point holds — and you already have an email list.
