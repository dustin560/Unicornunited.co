The Method

Four quadrants. One whole leader.

Not a personality test — a brand strategy session on you. Four lenses, mapped to a Johari-style window, surface the parts of your leadership the world already sees, the parts it doesn't, and the parts you don't yet know you have.

Quadrant 01

Light

Known to self and others — what you already illuminate

The qualities others openly affirm in you — captured from letters, testimonials, performance reviews, biographies and the words people use when they introduce you. The starting frame for what you'll amplify.

"Where do others already affirm you — and where do you affirm yourself?"

Quadrant 02

Dark

Unknown to self, seen by others — your blind-spot gifts

We interview your most trusted allies with one question — "what do you see in me when I'm at my best?" — and surface hidden qualities you can't see in yourself. The biggest source of unfair advantage in any personal brand.

"What surprising gift do others see in you that you've been undervaluing?"

Quadrant 03

Shadow

Known to self, hidden from others — what you conceal

The strengths and stories you suppress because they feel risky, niche, or "too much." Most leaders are leaking authority by hiding the very things that would make them magnetic. We help you reframe these as proof, not problem.

"What part of you have you been quieting that the world actually needs to hear?"

Quadrant 04

Source

Unknown to all — the future self pulling you forward

The territory of intuition, symbol, archetype and emerging direction. Using a structured archetypal profile and quiet reflection, we surface the leader you're becoming — before you're entirely sure yourself. The compass for the next decade.

"If your future self sent a message back, what would it say you were ready for?"

Questions, Answered

The questions leaders ask first.

Is this coaching?+
No. Coaching helps you become a better version of who you already are. Unicorn for Leaders is brand strategy — applied to a human. We're not your therapist; we're your strategic partner for becoming legible to the world.
How is this different from "personal branding"?+
Most personal branding is content tips. Unicorn for Leaders is brand strategy — the same craft we use for businesses — applied to the leader. You walk away with positioning, a POV, a story architecture and an activation plan, not just a content calendar.
Can it run alongside the Unicorn business engagement?+
That's where it works best. The company brand and the leader brand are two halves of the same equation, and running them together means the founder, the company and the market are telling one coherent story — not three competing ones.
What if I'm not the founder — I'm an exec or emerging leader?+
The method is the same; the application changes. We adapt the sprint or cohort to the moment in your career — promotion, transition, board portfolio, fractional practice, or moving into the next room you're meant to be in.
How long is the commitment?+
A sprint is 1–2 days. A cohort is 12 weeks. The fractional partnership runs on a 3-month minimum and continues for as long as it's the right thing. Most retainer relationships go a year or more.
Where do I start if I'm not sure which tier fits?+
Play the game. It's free, takes 25 minutes, and the recommendation it gives you at the end is the honest first take on which tier is right. From there we'll talk.
